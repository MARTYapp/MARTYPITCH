MARTY

Steady when the world isn’t.

MARTY is the voice-first emotional AI companion for quiet men, neurodivergent folks, and emotionally intelligent creatives.

Real-Time Demo

Try talking to MARTY. He's not here to fix you. Just to listen.

Vision

MARTY helps people feel seen — without clinical language or cartoon apps. Built to feel like a friend, not a product.

Problem

Men and creatives are underserved by mental health tech. Most tools feel gamified, stigmatizing, or cold. MARTY offers a new model.

Solution

A minimalist, emotionally fluent voice AI that understands mood, regulates emotion, and responds with real DBT-based care.

Wireframe Preview

Slide: Daily check-in → Journaling → Feedback Loop → DBT tool suggestion.

Business Model

Free: Journaling + quotes + grounding tools
Premium: Voice input + feedback + planner ($8.99/mo)
Enterprise: Licensing for HR, education, wellness

Exit Strategy

MARTY is optimized for acquisition or licensing: voice UX, DBT IP, and niche product-market fit. Potential targets: Notion, Headspace, Apple Health, Calm, BetterUp.
