The Emotional Infrastructure Gap

Millions of people are emotionally underserved by existing mental health solutions

The Therapy Problem

  • 67% of men avoid therapy due to stigma
  • Neurodivergent users find traditional therapy formats overwhelming
  • $200+ per session creates access barriers
  • Waiting lists stretch months

The App Problem

  • Wellness apps are designed for extroverts
  • Loud interfaces, pushy notifications
  • One-size-fits-all approaches fail neurodivergent users
  • No emotional continuity or learning

The Result

A generation of quiet minds—especially men and neurodivergent individuals—are left without emotional tools that actually fit their needs.
