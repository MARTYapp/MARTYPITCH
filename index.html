Vision

MARTY is the voice-first AI mental wellness app designed for quiet guys, neurodivergent users, and creative minds. It's not therapy — it's a steady companion when the world isn’t.

“Steady when the world isn’t.”

Problem

Most mental health tools are cold, clinical, or cartoonish. Quiet men, creatives, and neurodivergent users feel left behind — or never show up at all.

Solution

MARTY offers a calming voice-based journaling interface and emotional AI responses grounded in DBT. It's radically simple, emotionally intelligent, and never performative.
